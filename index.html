Introduction to SIH 2020!

What is Hack 4 India?

#Hack4India is the internal hackathon organised by RCCIIT, Kolkata for shortlisting teams for Smart India Hackathon 2020. Students intersted in developing software as well as Hardware solutions for the SIH problem statements can participate. It's a team competition. Students can also present their own unique idea under the Student Innovation category.
There are two tracks -: Software and Hardware
Top 5 teams in software and top 2 teams in hardware category will be shortlisted for the nominations for the Grand Finale of Smart India Hackathon 2020!
We encourage students from all streams and year of study to participate!

This is not an individual hackathon. So, you would require to form a team of 6 members consisting of atleast 1 female member. All members should be from RCCIIT only. Your team can consist of students from different branches and year of study.
